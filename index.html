INFO

You can use either mouse wheel or buttons on bottom & top of each slider for scrolling.
Right bike2 is compared to left bike1. Comparison results are displayed in the most right column.
Arrow points upward if bike2' value is bigger than bike1'. If value is worse, arrow becomes red, if good - green.
For example, if right bike is more expensive, arrow will point upward & become red. Because if the bike is more expensive, value is bigger, but it's harder to buy it. Simple as that.
Every time you change bike in main menu, it becomes left active bike in comparison menu. Right active bike automatically being switched to next more expensive bike to left active one.
To exit comparison menu, just click wherever outside of comparison menu window.
